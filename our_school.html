About Us

Learn about our school's mission, history, and values.

New Vision & New Kyungdong

A place of learning traditional ideology that realizes true education.

Foundation Philosophy

Under the philosophy of humanitarianism, we nurture the right personality and talents as democratic citizens while promoting creative human resources who will contribute to the development of the nation and the realization of the prosperity of humanity.

Educational Goals

  • A profound understanding of life and the world through objective thinking and the fulfillment of an amicable character.
  • Succession and development of traditional culture and thoughts through an independent National Education Program.
  • Fostering talents who lead modernization, science, globalization, and informatization through advanced professional education.

KDU Spirit

Together with fidelity, filial duty, benevolence, and respect, which are all important characteristics of Korean people, and the practical attitude of ceaseless endeavors, we lead society in V3 capable person and volunteer work.

Motto

Creativity (창의)

Self-discipline (자립)

Service to fellowmen (봉사)

Who KDU Looks For

  • V3 type capable person
  • Persons who are gifted with virtues (Personality)
  • Persons who are prepared with values (Professionalism)
  • Persons who are achievers of vocation (Practice)
  • Personality: Leadership
  • Professionalism: Rational thinking, Global communication
  • Practice: Self-discipline, Social contribution

Vision

Convergence of Innovation and Creativity in Higher Education Upholding Virtuous Characteristics and Clear Vision.

Mission

Nurturing capable professionals with V3 (Virtues, Values, Vocation) strengthening the Employment Academy status.

Strategic Goals

  • Establishing a field-oriented education system to foster talented students for the 4th industrial revolution.
  • Strengthening local industry-associated academic functions and industrial research capabilities.
  • Establishing characterized and shared growth of the community.
  • Operating a customized innovative management system for trainees.
