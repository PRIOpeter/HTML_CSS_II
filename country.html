Welcome to South Korea

Discover the vibrant culture, advanced technology, and rich history of South Korea.

Key Cities and Regions

  • Seoul: The capital city with skyscrapers, palaces, and street markets.
  • Gangwon Province: Home to Kyungdong University, Nami Island, and Seoraksan National Park.
  • Busan: Coastal city known for beaches and seafood.
  • Incheon: International airport and historical sites.
  • Jeju: Island with volcanic landscapes and beaches.

Transportation and Living Near Kyungdong University

Living near Kyungdong University in Sokcho offers great convenience. The area is well-connected and provides a rich cultural experience.

Transportation

Sokcho’s public bus system connects the city and nearby areas like Seoraksan. Buses from the Sokcho Intercity Bus Terminal reach major cities such as Seoul, Busan, and Incheon. The nearest train station is in Gangneung, 30 minutes away by bus. Taxis are widely available, especially near the beach.

Living in Bongpo-ri

Bongpo-ri offers apartments ranging from high-rises to family homes, with amenities like parking and fitness centers. The peaceful environment is ideal for students and families.

Supermarkets and Convenience Stores

Sokcho has supermarkets like E-Mart and Homeplus, local stores for fresh produce, and 24/7 convenience stores like 7-Eleven and CU.

Beach Atmosphere

Sokcho’s beaches like Naksan and Sokcho Beach offer a relaxed atmosphere with swimming, sunbathing, and cafes, along with scenic views of the East Sea and Seoraksan.
